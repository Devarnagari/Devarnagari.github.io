Rules of Tunnel Tennis

Tunnel tennis is a game normally played in a inclosed net. The game requies two players. The equipment needed is 2 tennis rackets and 1 ball. It is played in a inclosed net with a artificial turf it needs to be dry so the ball bounces. The rules of the game are

  • 1.Everytime the ball stops for more than 2 seconds the other player gets one point
  • 2.Every time the ball crosses half way it is counted as the other players ball
  • 3.Don't hit the ball as hard as you can or else...
  • 4.When a player gets to 5 points they win
